\begin{center}
  \large{ON NOTATION AND TERMINOLOGY}
\end{center}
Unless otherwise specified, we use the following notations and terminologies throughout the thesis.
\begin{align*}
  &\Re &\text{ the set of real numbers}, \\
  &L &\text{ the loss function}, \\
  &\T &\text{ the transpose of a matrix}, \\
  &w &\text{ a member of a vocabulary (usually a word)}, \\
  &V=\{w_{1}, w_{2}, \dots, w_{|V|}\} &\text{ a (finite) vocabulary}, \\
  &\mathbf{w} &\text{ the vector representation of $w$}, \\
  &\mathbf{w}(w) &\text{ the vector representation of $w$}, \\
\end{align*}
